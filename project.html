New Regent Cafe (Web Design)

This website was made for a Hong Kong style cafe that is located in San Francisco's Chinatown. 90% of the restaurant's customers are Chinese immigrants, therefore these diners mainly speak Chinese. The site enable customers to receive updates on the restaurant's holiday hours and their weekly specials. In addition, individuals can navigate through the business's contact information and breakfast/lunch menus.

INFO 190: Incarcerated with Schizophrenia (Data Analysis)

By chronicling the journey of offenders who have gone through a myriad of systems, the solution provides a comprehensive look into the rise of high-utilization. Navigating through each tab, the user learns about the behavior and demographics of these coined high-utilizers at the system level. Ultimately, the website allows individuals to uncover intel at the intersection of the mental health crisis and the aforementioned systems through the case of schizo-related disorders.
